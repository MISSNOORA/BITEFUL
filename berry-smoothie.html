Berry Banana Smoothie

A quick and refreshing smoothie made with fresh fruits, perfect for busy mornings.

Lina Omar
Smoothies

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve.
